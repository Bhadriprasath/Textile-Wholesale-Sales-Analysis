# 📊 Project Summary

## Overview

This project was developed to transform a Business Requirement Document into a fully functional analytical solution using Power BI.

The goal was to create measurable KPIs that address real-world business risks and growth opportunities within the textile wholesale industry.

# Business Challenges Addressed

* Revenue dependency on top retailers

* Seasonal and fluctuating demand

* Margin visibility across products

* Limited customer lifecycle tracking

* Regional performance variation

## Analytical Framework

The project follows a layered KPI approach:

* Base Measures (Sales, Cost, Profit)

* Retailer Lifecycle Measures

* Performance KPIs

* Risk & Concentration Metrics

* Growth & Time Intelligence Metrics

## Outcome

The final dashboard enables stakeholders to:

* Track profitability trends

* Identify revenue concentration risks

* Monitor customer loyalty

* Evaluate product contribution margins

* Analyze month-on-month growth
